Post-graduate department of commerce

Department of Commerce (P.G) started in the year 2013, is enriched with 7 Lecturers which offers Masters and Bachelors degree in Commerce-M.Com (Finance) and B.Com (Taxation). These courses are considered to be the most thriving courses in the field of Education. It helps the students gain, theoretical and practical skills needed to deal with real life business situations and transform themselves into young entrepreneurs in this fast changing innovative world. The course also enhances students with skill and knowledge in verbal and interpersonal communication, critical thinking, time management and problem solving skills.

courses offered

    • Masters of Commerce (M.com Finance)
    • Bachelors of Commerce (B.com Taxation)
    • Bachelors of Commerce (B.com Capital Market)
